Tracy Jones MD, MPH

Tracy Jones, MD, MPH is a bi-coastal psychiatrist and medical expert based in New York City and Los Angeles. A sought-after guest on news and talk shows, she has appeared on live TV over 50 times in the past year.

Dr. Jones started her medical journalism career in ABC’s News Medical Unit Resident Program. She worked alongside producers and chief medical correspondent and co-host of GMA3, Dr. Jennifer Ashton. Yalda was responsible for editing and writing medical scripts and authored articles on the latest health topics on ABC News and Good Morning America.

Today, she is part of a solid regular lineup of medical guests delivering health information for a variety of networks—including ABC News, Good Morning America, Fox News, NewsNation, Court TV, and Dr. Phil.
